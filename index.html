Award winning UI/UX Designer based in Sydney

“The future belongs to those who learn more skills and combine them in creative ways”

— Robert Green, Mastery

French award-winning designer currently living in Sydney, AU. Designer and self-taught UX, Front-end developer, I’m a creative person who's passionate about making the best possible experience for people. I'm also Judge @ CSSDA

With 6 years of experience in design, I won a few awards like

  • 2 #WOTD at CSSDesignAwards in 2015 & 2012
  • 2 CSSAwards Featured in 2015 & 2012
  • 2 Honorables Mentions at Awwwards
  • #WOTD at CSSWinner
  • Bemyapp Games WorldCup
  • Dailymotion Best Mobile Application
  • France National's 1st Prize of BeMyApp WorldCup
  • Winner #101projets and 1st Prize off Pernod-Ricard BreakTheCode.

I had the privilege to work with huge brands like Airtasker, Snappr, L'Oreal, Nespresso, Michelin, Schneider Electrics, Badoit, Laposte Mobile, Sanofi, Celio, Intel, Credit Agricole, Hana, Lyxumia and many others...

Currently leading the design at KeyPay. Always looking for new interaction.
